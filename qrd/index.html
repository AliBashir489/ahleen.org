What is the Quran Revision Day?

The Quran Revision Day is an initiative taken to provide the people of the Quran with a complete day where they do not have to worry about anything except the Quran. It is an opportunity to take a break from the daily routines and concentrate on revising the different portions of the Quran.

Additionally, the spiritually rich environment will allow the brothers to share the company of local scholars and each other's virtuous company. Furthermore, we pray to Allah that it will encourage those that are currently engaged in memorization and/or learning Allah's Words to devote more time.

We pray to Allah for acceptance of this event for the propagation of the Quran and to benefit all of those who participate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I haven't memorized the entire Qur'an?

+

Sign up for the tier that best fits your level. If you have one juz memorized and ready, join the corresponding tier. Similarly, if you have 12 ajzaa, opt for the 12-juz tier. Strive to excel and inspire before you expire.

What if my Qur'an memorization is weak?

+

A believer should always remain positive with Allah, the Almighty, and have lofty intentions and aspirations. The Prophet Ô∑∫ said: "Actions are according to their intentions." Start with the smallest step‚Äîsign up for the 1-juz tier and make it your goal to strengthen that portion before the event. Trust in His mercy, and He will open doors for you that you never imagined possible.

Will participants receive prizes or gifts?

+

Yes, participants will be given gifts based on their respective tiers. Those in the higher tiers will receive more significant prizes to encourage and support their continued preparation and dedication.

Will accommodation be provided?

+

Sleeping arrangements will be provided inside the masjid for brothers (over 18) who require them. Brothers are requested to bring their own bedding.

Will food be provided?

+

Food arrangements have been made for the duration of the program. Individuals with allergies can inform the organizers through the registration form, and adequate arrangements will be made.

How much will it cost?

+

The event is free of charge (contingent upon your participation). Participants are required to pay a fully refundable $25 fee through the registration form to reserve their spot. The total amount will be refunded when the participant arrives at the venue.
